It is **not** a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and does not replace professional verification.

Because the App relies on machine-learning image recognition:

- Results can be **wrong even when they appear confident**.
- The model may **confuse visually similar items**, including different medications.
- Accuracy depends on image conditions and may degrade without warning.
- **You, not PillCount, are responsible for detecting and correcting errors** in App output.
